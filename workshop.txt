description=[h1]This is my first public mod created for personal use on a private server, not sure about permissions and credits work on PZ Workshop but here's what this mod does:[/h1]
description=[h2]This is a development branch, github repro below, feel free to submit commits, especially for mod support and localization[/h2]
description=
description=Using Ammunition Box & Crate, patached with Brita/Arsenal26, using standard weights of ammoboxes, a case of 10 boxes reduces the weight of 10 boxes by 10% when put into a case:
description=[code]
description=1 Box of 9x19mm = 0.5 * 10 to make a case = 5.0
description=5.0 * 0.90 = 4.5
description=10% weight reduction on Case of 9x19mm Ammo Boxes x 10
description=[/code]
description=
description=
description=I also standarded all magazines, individual bullets, and ammo boxes into the "Ammo" display category as well as giving them ammo names their real life counterpart names (mostly because myself and friends are gun owners/nerds):
description=
description=[code]
description=5.56 > 5.56x45mm NATO
description=5.7x28mm > 5.7x28mm NATO
description=7.62x51 > 7.62x51mm NATO
description=9mm > 9x19mm
description=[/code]
description=
description=Ammo & Mag Exchange allow for conversion of things like:
description=[code]2x30rnd 5.56 STANAG > 60rnd 5.56x45 Drum
description=[/code]
